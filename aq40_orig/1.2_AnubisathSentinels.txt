1. Heal over time

5. Shadowbolt Volley (15 yard range)
6. Reflect Shadow/Frost
7. Reflect Fire/Arcane
8. Thunderclap
9. Thorns

1. 1 tank per mob
2. Detect magic on each mob
3. Kill the mobs with easy abilites first
